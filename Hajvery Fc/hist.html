History of FC Hajvery

Founded in 2023

FC Hajvery was established with the aim of promoting football in the community. It was founded by a group of local kids who shared a deep passion for the game. They often played together in their neighborhood, enjoying the thrill of competition and the joy of teamwork. Realizing their love for football could be transformed into something more, they decided to form a team that would represent them as a united club. The creation of FC Hajvery not only serves as a platform for these young athletes to showcase their skills but also encourages more kids in the area to participate in sports. Football can be a powerful tool for building friendships, teaching discipline, and instilling a sense of responsibility. The sense of belonging and camaraderie that comes from being part of a team can have lasting positive effects on the players' lives, both on and off the field. In this way, FC Hajvery is more than just a football team; it is a beacon of hope and unity for the local community.

Our Goal

As a futsal club, our goal is to foster a love for the game while promoting skill development and teamwork among our players. We aim to create an inclusive environment where everyone, regardless of their skill level, can participate, learn, and grow. We focus on enhancing individual abilities through practice and competition, emphasizing technical skills, tactical understanding, and physical fitness. Our club also seeks to build strong relationships among players, coaches, and families, creating a supportive community that encourages personal and athletic development. Additionally, we aspire to promote the values of sportsmanship, respect, and dedication, both on and off the court. By participating in local leagues and tournaments, we want to showcase our talent and commitment to futsal, while also inspiring others in the community to get involved in the sport. Ultimately, our goal is to cultivate a passion for futsal that lasts a lifetime and contributes positively to our community.
